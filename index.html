How to Play:

  • Enter a number in the field below.
  • Your number will appear in a list at the bottom of the page.

There are a few exceptions to how your number will appear on the page:

  • If you number is divisible by 3 it will become "ping"
  • If your number is divisible by 5 it will become "pong"
  • If your number is divisble by 3 and 5 it will become "pingpong"
